Privacy Policy

Qazyna — Personal Finance App  ·  Last updated: April 2026

✓ Your data stays on your device

📱 Local Data Storage

Qazyna stores all your financial data locally on your device only using SQLite. We do not operate servers that store your personal financial information.

Data stored locally on your device:

  • Account names and balances
  • Income and expense transactions
  • Debt records and reminders
  • Categories and financial goals
  • PIN code (stored as a hash, never in plain text)
All your financial data is deleted permanently when you uninstall the app. We have no copy.

🤖 AI Features (Optional)

When you use AI categorization or the AI financial advisor, anonymized transaction names are sent to Anthropic Claude API for processing. Personal financial totals, account names, and identifying information are not included in these requests.

Anthropic processes this data according to their privacy policy: anthropic.com/privacy

AI features are optional. The app works fully without them.

🎤 Voice Input (Optional)

When you use voice input, your audio recording is sent to OpenAI Whisper API for speech-to-text transcription. The audio is not stored by Qazyna after transcription.

OpenAI processes this data according to their privacy policy: openai.com/privacy

You can use the app without ever granting microphone access.

🔔 Notifications

Qazyna uses local push notifications only — for debt payment reminders and financial alerts you configure yourself. These notifications are generated entirely on your device.

No notification content or data is sent to external servers.

💱 Exchange Rates

To display currency exchange rates, the app fetches public rate data from the National Bank of Kazakhstan (nationalbank.kz). This is a read-only request — no personal data is sent.

🔒 Security

Qazyna supports PIN code and biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID) to protect access to your financial data. These security features operate entirely on your device using iOS security APIs.

📦 Third-Party SDKs

The app is built with Expo SDK. Expo may collect anonymous crash reports and diagnostic data to improve stability. See Expo's privacy policy: expo.dev/privacy

👶 Children's Privacy

Qazyna is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
