# Privacy Policy — Qazyna

**Last updated: March 31, 2026**

## What data we collect

Qazyna stores all your financial data **locally on your device only**. We do not have servers that store your personal financial information.

**Data stored locally:**
- Account names and balances
- Income and expense transactions
- Debt records
- Financial goals and limits

**Data sent to third parties:**
- **Voice input** (optional): Audio is sent to OpenAI Whisper for transcription. OpenAI's privacy policy applies.
- **AI features** (optional): Transaction names are sent to Anthropic Claude for categorization. No personal financial totals are shared.
- **Exchange rates**: We fetch currency rates from the National Bank of Kazakhstan (nationalbank.kz). No personal data is sent.

## Microphone

We request microphone access only when you use the voice input feature. You can use the app without ever granting microphone access.

## Data deletion

All your data is deleted when you uninstall the app. We have no copy.
